Operation codes. 


operand types:
    

  • Z: no operand (Z,Z,Z,Z)
    • 

  • B: 8bit (B,S,B,B)
    • 

  • BB: 8+8bit (BB,SB,BS,SS)
    • 

  • BBB: 8+8+8bit (BBB,SBB,BSB,SSB)
    • 

  • BS: 8+16bit (BS,SS,BS,BS)
    • 

  • S: 16bit (S,S,S,S)
    • 

  • W: 24bit (W,W,W,W) 
    •
